Understanding Rackspace CDN
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
Rackspace CDN gives you the power to accelerate content on any
public resource at Rackspace. It provides a simple API and
Control Panel experience for you to manage your CDN-enabled domains
and the origins and assets associated with those domains.
